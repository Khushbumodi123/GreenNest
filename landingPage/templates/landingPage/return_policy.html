Return Policy

At GreenNest, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, you can return the product under the following conditions.

Eligibility for Returns

Items must be returned within 30 days of receipt.

Items must be unused, in the same condition that you received them, and in their original packaging.

Some items, such as perishable goods, custom products, or personal care items, are not eligible for return.

Return Process

1. Contact our customer service team at [contact email] to initiate a return.

2. Provide your order number, a description of the item you wish to return, and the reason for the return.

3. Our team will review your request and, if approved, will provide a return authorization and shipping instructions.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within a certain amount of days.

Shipping Costs

If the return is a result of our error (you received an incorrect or defective item), we will cover the return shipping costs.

For other returns, you will be responsible for paying the return shipping costs. Shipping costs are non-refundable.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding returns, please contact us or visit our Contact Us page.
